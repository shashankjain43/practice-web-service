For Physical Store

Who should use FreeCharge offline payment solution?

Our freecharge offline payment solution is ideal for merchants who have physical stores, e.g, retail chains, supermarkets, restaurants. Consumers can transact using their FreeCharge wallet at any FreeCharge enabled partner store.

Why should one use it?

  1. Simple and fast integration for any business size
  2. Quick and easy payment method
  3. All that Customer needs is FreeCharge app and account for #OnTheGoPin to pay
  4. Works even for users without data or network connectivity
  5. 30+ million accounts

How to use it?

FreeCharge offline payments work as follows

  • Step 1: FreeCharge customer enters registered mobile number
  • Step 2: Customer enters dynamically generated #OnTheGoPin to authorise the payment
  • Step 3: FreeCharge authenticates and completes the transaction
